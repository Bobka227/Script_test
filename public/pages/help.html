Help and Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the FoodMood Help section! Here you will find answers to the most frequently asked questions about using our website. If you need further assistance, don't hesitate to contact us through the contact information provided below.

  • How can I register on FoodMood?

    To register, click the "Sign In/Sign Up" button at the top of the page. Then fill in your details and create an account.

  • Is using FoodMood free?

    Yes, the basic features of FoodMood are completely free. You can register and start exploring recipes at no cost.

  • How can I add my own recipe?

    To add your own recipe, you first need to log in. Then go to the "Food Recipes" section and click on "Add Recipe."

  • What types of recipes can I find on FoodMood?

    On FoodMood, you can find a wide range of recipes, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and many other categories tailored to your preferences.

  • How can I create a daily meal plan based on my mood?

    Go to the "Mood Recipes" section and select your current mood. FoodMood will automatically recommend a daily meal plan that suits you.

  • Can I save my favorite recipes?

    Yes, log in to your account and click the "Save" button next to the recipe you want to save to your favorites list.

  • How can I share recipes with friends?

    You can share recipes by clicking the share icon under a recipe. Recipes can be shared via email or on social networks like Facebook or Twitter.

  • What should I do if I have issues registering?

    If you have issues registering, contact us at support@FoodMood.com, and we'll be happy to help.

  • What are the benefits of creating an account?

    By creating an account, you gain access to saving favorite recipes, creating your own recipes, and customizing daily meal plans based on your mood and preferences.

  • Can I search for recipes by ingredients?

    Yes, you can use the search bar on the "Food Recipes" page and enter the ingredients you have to find matching recipes.

About FoodMood

FoodMood is your personal food assistant, helping you explore new recipes and customize your meal plans according to your mood. Discover delicious recipes, whether you're happy, sad, or anything in between!
